# Agriculture and Mineral & Energy Resources - MCQs

### **Agriculture**

1. Which type of farming is known as 'slash and burn' agriculture?
   - [ ] A. Plantation agriculture
   - [ ] B. Intensive subsistence farming
   - [x] C. Primitive subsistence farming
   - [ ] D. Commercial farming

   <details>
   <summary>Answer & Explanation</summary>
   **Answer:** C. Primitive subsistence farming
   **Explanation:** 'Slash and burn' agriculture, also known as jhumming, is a type of primitive subsistence farming where land is cleared by burning vegetation and cultivated for a few years before being abandoned.
   </details>
