## 1. Executive Summary <a name="executive-summary"></a>

OmniVast Technologies is a **venture studio** building AI-first products for India’s next billion users. Our core philosophy is **"Edge-First AI"**, focusing on optimizing lightweight AI models (e.g., LiteRT, Gemma 2, Llama 3) to run locally on mid-range devices. This reduces operational costs and enables deployment in data-scarce regions (e.g., Rural India).

### Portfolio Overview
| Product         | Sector          | Target Audience                     | Unique Selling Proposition (USP)                                                                 |
|-----------------|-----------------|-------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Discipline OS   | EdTech          | College students (18–25 years)     | AI-powered DSA tracker with offline explanations, no ads, and gamification.                     |
| Syntara         | Social          | Gen Z/millennials (18–35 years)     | Music-based social networking with vibe-matching AI and synchronous listening rooms.           |
| SuvyAIkth       | Rural EdTech    | Rural school children (6–16 years) | Offline AI education with SMS-based queries via Raspberry Pi + GSM.                            |
| Fitnessy        | FemTech         | Women (18–45 years)                 | Indian diet-focused calorie tracking, period tracking, and AI-driven mood journal.               |
| CDSS            | MedTech         | Doctors (25–55 years)               | European-standard AI diagnostics with multi-model integration (Claude Opus, Gemini 2.0, Imagen 4).|

**Mission:** Bridge gaps in education, healthcare, and social connectivity using affordable, scalable AI.

#### Monetization
- **Syntara Volume Play:** We dropped the monthly price from **₹199** to **₹79**. 
- Goal: We now need **3x more users** to hit similar revenue targets. 
- Tactic: Marketing must pivot to *"Cheaper than a Chai"* messaging.
- **Micro-transactions:** Added revenue stream from `"pay-per-use"` features (e.g., "Send a Song Dedication" for ₹5). 
- This captures revenue from users who refuse to subscribe but will impulse-buy. 
- **Competitive Alignment:** The new price (₹79) sits comfortably between Spotify Student (₹59) and Spotify Individual (₹119). It no longer feels `"overpriced"` compared to the music source itself.

| **Value Sub** (Monthly)  | **₹79 / month**                                | **₹9,48,000** <br>*(Based on 2,000 loyal monthly users)* |
