### **Spring Boot Group ID Naming Conventions**

- **`com`** – For **commercial companies** (e.g., `com.google`, `com.amazon.app`)
- **`org`** – For **open-source or organizations** (e.g., `org.springframework`, `org.apache`)
- **`net`** – For **network-based or alternate use** (e.g., `net.sf.hibernate`)
