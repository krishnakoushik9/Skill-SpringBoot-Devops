- **Origin**: The MVC pattern was first introduced by **Trygve Reenskaug** in **1979** while working on Smalltalk-79 at Xerox PARC (Palo Alto Research Center).
    
- **Purpose**: It was designed to **separate internal representations of information** from the ways that information is presented to and accepted from the user.
    
- Over time, it became widely adopted in **web development** frameworks such as **Ruby on Rails, Django (Python), Laravel (PHP), ASP.NET (C#), Spring (Java)** and front-end frameworks like **Angular**.
